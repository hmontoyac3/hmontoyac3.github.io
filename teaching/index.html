Redesigning Managerial Skills for All Bachelor Students

In collaboration with BUILT · Bocconi University · Ongoing

Working with BUILT (Bocconi University) and faculty to redesign the Managerial Skills seminar at scale — for the full undergraduate population. The redesign integrates AI tools more deeply into every session while keeping a clear pedagogical line: AI can help generate presentations, websites, and materials, but it is the student who must present, convince, and negotiate. Human skills are non-negotiable; AI is the scaffold, not the substitute.
