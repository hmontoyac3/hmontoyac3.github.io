SDM Instrument
With Arnaldo Camuffo · Target: Research Policy

Is scientific decision-making an individual trait or an organizational one? Using ICC variance decomposition on a sample of 7,786 managers across 50+ firms, this paper establishes SDM as a stable individual-level capability — the measurement foundation for a broader research agenda on decision-making under uncertainty.

Heterogeneous Effects of Theory-Based Training
With Alfonso Gambardella · IdeaBoosterLab RCT data

Theory-based entrepreneurship training works on average — but for whom? This paper proposes a systematic taxonomy of heterogeneous treatment effects organized into three categories: person-level, venture-level, and dosage. Using random forest for exploration and HTE regressions across 5 pooled RCTs, it provides the first systematic account of which entrepreneur and startup characteristics moderate training response.
